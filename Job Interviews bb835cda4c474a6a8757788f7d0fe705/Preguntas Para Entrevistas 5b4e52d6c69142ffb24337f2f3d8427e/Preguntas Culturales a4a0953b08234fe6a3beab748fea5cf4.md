# Preguntas Culturales

- Customer Orientation
    
    Ask, for a specific example where he / she demonstrated pro activeness to enhance the value for the customer.
    
    1. Ask for a specific example when the candidate in the past had provided exceptional customer service far beyond the expectations of the customers.
    2. Give a project scenario, and check, whether the candidate is able to see the big picture.
    3. If the candidate is leading a team, on a new project, how will he / she ensure that the entire team is completely focused as a solution provider for the customer? How will he / she nurture the team for ensuring enhanced commitment from everybody?
    4. How can the candidate demonstrate to the customer that he / she can be relied upon? Ask for examples.
    5. He / she is leading a project and the project fails. Who is responsible? Hear the answer first. Then ask, whether, he / she should personal responsibility for the same, and if that is done isnâ€™t there a greater probability that it would not have failed in the first place.
    6. Has the candidate ever measured customer satisfaction? Ask for specific example, and get in to details.
    7. What in your experience/ knowledge are some of the best ways to enhance the engagement levels with the customers. Ask for some past examples. Get in to details.
- Attitude
    1. Ask, what are their strengths and weaknesses. After this, first ask what the candidate has done in the past six months to correct his / her weaknesses. Then ask, what has he / she done to enhance his / her strengths in the corresponding period. Check for the passion; where does it lie. Is the passion in weakness correction or is sitting in strength enhancements. Have a discussion on how has he / she has identified his / her strengths, and what does he / she think is better: strengths enhancement or weakness correction. This discussion may be the emphasis on strengths enhancement or on weakness correction.
    2. Now, ask whether he / she is aware of the key strengths and weaknesses of his/ her team mates. Ask for a specific example of how he / she has tried in the past to leverage the strengths of his / her team mates.
    3. Have you ever faced a seriously uphill task / crisis in your life so far? How did you come out of it? What were the lessons learnt? After this ask, do you think that you emerged stronger after this? Probe and try to understand how resilient the person is?
    4. Given the choice between 6 very intelligent / skilled / and experienced but negative persons, as against 6 persons of average skills / intelligence / experience who are highly positive, whom would you select and why?
    5. Once the candidate suggests that he / she will make the latter choice then try to understand the reason for it and see to what extent the person is passionately discussing about Positive Attitudes, Hope, and Optimism etc.
    6. What is your belief; can the positivity or negativity of a person be changed? Have you ever had the experience of changing it? If yes, get a detailed description.
    7. What are some of the things that you think can be done for enhancing the well-being of the team. Check for the emphasis of internal versus internal.
- Communication Skills
    1. Was the person listening to you carefully and patiently? Or was he / she jumping to answer even before really allow to complete your questions? Assess this very judiciously because the candidates at times can do this mistake just because of enthusiasm.
    2. Were the answers suggesting evidence of carefully organized thoughts? Was he / she able to give a coherent/ well organized account? You can assess this based on the questions where you had asked for any personal example.
    3. Was the person answering your questions in a cheerful manner? Was he / she able to establish a good connection with you?
    4. How was the body language? Was he / she looking interested or bored?
    5. Whenever given a chance, was the candidate able to answer in a logical manner?
    6. Was he / she able to present complex things in a simple manner?
    7. Was the candidate energetic in his / her overall expression?
    8. How would you rate the candidate on a given scale in terms of his / her knowledge and capacity to speak / write in English?
- TEAM EFFECTIVENESS
    1. What motivates you more: working in an individual situation or in a team situation, and why?
    2. How would you try to find your way in a new team where other existing members are not favorably disposed towards you?
    3. If you prefer a team situation for working, what are some of the key learning that you have taken from your experience of working in teams? Cite some concrete examples from the past, where you learnt something from a team situation.
    4. Have you ever had an opportunity of conflict resolution? Please narrate that example.
    5. You are not in the best of mood/ frame of mind/ health or are preoccupied otherwise. Is it reasonable for you to expect that your team members will help you by taking off some of your burdens during such phases? Is it fair on your part to expect this? Would you extend answers.
